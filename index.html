Arun Sharma

Hi there! I am a thrid year Ph.D. student at the Spatial Computing Research Group, within the Computer Science Department at University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. I am interested in Data Mining, Machine Learning and High Performance Computing. I am advised by Prof. Shashi Shekhar. I am also fortunate to be funded by National Geospatial Intelligence Agency.

Prior to UMN, I did my masters at University at Buffalo in Computer Science, where I was affiliated with UB Data Science Research Lab (UBDS) and worked under Prof. Varun Chandola on problems based on big data and spatiotemporal data mining. I've also received my Bachelors and Masters in Computer Science at Gautam Buddha University, India.
